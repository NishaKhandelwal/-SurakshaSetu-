# 📄 SurakshaSetu Insurance Policy

### Income Protection Policy for Delivery Workers

---

## 1. Policy Overview

SurakshaSetu is an AI-powered parametric insurance policy designed to protect delivery workers from **loss of income due to environmental disruptions** such as extreme weather and pollution.

This policy provides **automatic payouts** when predefined conditions are met, without requiring manual claims.

---

## 2. Eligibility

A user is eligible if they:

* Are an active food delivery partner
* Work at least **15 hours per week**
* Enable GPS tracking during working hours
* Have a valid UPI-linked bank account

---

## 3. Coverage (What is Covered)

This policy covers **income loss only** caused by:

* Heavy rainfall or flooding 🌧️
* Extreme heat (heatwaves) ☀️
* Severe air pollution 🌫️

Coverage applies only when these events directly reduce working hours and earnings.

---

## 4. Exclusions (What is NOT Covered)

This policy does NOT cover:

* Health or medical expenses
* Accidents or injuries
* Vehicle damage or repairs
* Account suspension or bans
* Voluntary absence from work

---

## 5. Policy Duration

* Valid for **1 week (Monday–Sunday)**
* Automatically renewable upon weekly premium payment

---

## 6. Premium Structure

Premium is calculated on a **weekly basis** using AI-based risk assessment.

### Formula:

```
Weekly Premium = Base + Weather Risk + City Risk - Reliability Discount
```

### Typical Range:

* ₹40 – ₹100 per week depending on risk level

---

## 7. Parametric Triggers

Payouts are triggered automatically when these conditions are met:

| Event        | Trigger Condition  |
| ------------ | ------------------ |
| Heavy Rain   | Rainfall > 80 mm   |
| Extreme Rain | Rainfall > 120 mm  |
| Heatwave     | Temperature > 45°C |
| Pollution    | AQI > 400          |

---

## 8. Claim & Payout Process

This is a **parametric policy**, so no manual claim is required.

### Process:

1. System detects disruption
2. Verifies worker activity
3. Confirms income drop
4. Sends payout instantly via UPI

---

## 9. Payout Calculation

Payout is based on income loss during the affected shift.

### Example:

* Expected income: ₹700
* Actual income: ₹200
* Loss: ₹500

Coverage: **30–40%**

👉 Final payout: ₹150–₹200

---

## 10. Fraud Detection

To ensure fairness, the system uses:

* GPS location validation
* Activity tracking
* Duplicate claim detection
* Anomaly detection

Invalid claims are automatically rejected.

---

## 11. Conditions

* Users must provide accurate information
* GPS must remain enabled during working hours
* Fraudulent activity may lead to policy cancellation
* System triggers are final and binding
